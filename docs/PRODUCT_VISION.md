# LIT ATLAS Product Vision

## Product

LIT ATLAS is an Enterprise AI Operating System powered by its AI engine, LITGen.

## Mission

Help organizations accelerate Leadership, Innovation and Transformation by aligning workforce capability with business strategy through AI-assisted intelligence and human decision-making.

## Primary Users and Stakeholders

**Primary users**

- HR Leaders
- Learning & Development Leaders

**Stakeholders**

- CEOs
- Business Leaders
- Department Heads

## Core Problem

Organizations struggle to identify capability gaps, recommend the right learning interventions, monitor employee implementation after training, measure business impact, demonstrate ROI, and build a continuous learning culture.

## Product Principles

1. **Business Strategy First** — Every recommendation begins with business objectives.
2. **KSA-Based Competency** — Capability development is grounded in Knowledge, Skills, and Attitudes.
3. **Bloom's Taxonomy** — Learning pathways and assessments align with Bloom's Taxonomy.
4. **Human-in-the-Loop** — AI recommends; humans approve.
5. **AI Augments Humans** — AI supports decisions; humans remain accountable.

## North Star Outcome

Organizations continuously build the workforce capabilities required to achieve business strategy through personalized AI-assisted development, measurable progress, and human-led decision-making.

## Initial Market Entry

Learning & Development Intelligence is LIT ATLAS's first module. LIT ATLAS remains an Enterprise AI Operating System.
